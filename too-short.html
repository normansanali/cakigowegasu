Too Short Biography

Too Short is best known as Rapper, actor, record producer who has an estimated Net Worth of $15 Million. Influential West Coast rapper who released fifteen albums between 1987 and 2012. He is referred to as one of the pioneers of West Coast hip hop and was one of the first rappers to use explicit lyrics about pimping in his songs. He was born on April 28,1966, Los Angeles, CA. He had his greatest commercial success with the two-time Platinum album Life Is…Too Short in 1988. He released his first song, “Don't Stop Rappin,” at age seventeen. He featured the vocals of Lil Kim on his song “Call Me.” He raised a daughter named Octavia with his wife Erica Escarcega. His zodiac sign is Taurus
