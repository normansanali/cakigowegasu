Kelly Sheridan Voice

Sheridan plays Straight Glimmer, Sassy Saddles, Misty Fly, and Indigo Zap. Before being cast as Starlight Glimmer, Sheridan had “tried for years” to get a role in the series, having auditioned for all of the main characters. When notified about the role of Starlight by her agent, Sheridan recorded an audition in her hotel room while on a layover in Kuala Lumpur.

In interviews, Sheridan has described her voice direction for Starlight Glimmer as having not a trace of her sinister nature come through until the episode’s climax. In previous generations of My Little Pony, Sheridan provided the voices of Melody in My Little Pony Tales and various characters in several G3 animated specials, including Cotton Candy and Cheerilee.

Outside My Little Pony, Sheridan is best known for voicing Barbie in the majority of the Barbie animated films since 2001, except a few films made between 2010 and 2012 where she was replaced by Diana Kaarina, the voice of Aria Blaze.
