ERICO Bellisima Hotel, Egbeda

Erico bellisima hotel is a budget hotel located beside Tantalizers by Sobo Bus stop, Egbeda. Rooms available include single, standard, deluxe and royal suites.

All rooms have air conditioners, king sized beds and pillows, work tables and an LCD television set with cable TV connection. Rooms also have en-suite bathrooms which are kept clean by housekeeping staff.

The restaurant of Erico Bellisima hotel offers a variety of local and intercontinental cuisines prepared daily by a well-trained cooking staff, the room service option is also available for those who wish to have their meals delivered to their room. The bar is fully stock with soft drinks, juices, wines and alcoholic beverages that are served at a very chilled temperature.

Guests can enjoy swimming in the Erico bellisima hotel swimming pool. This is the perfect place for guests to lay back and relax as there is a poolside bar fully stocked with various drinks. With a very tight security service, vehicles are not parked at owners’ risk, they are watched and securely guarded 24hours of the day at the special car parking space provided. Round the clock electricity is guaranteed with the hotel’s standby generator.

Erico bellisima hotel is a walking distance from a nearby catholic church and its location makes it very easy to connect further on towards the Lekki- Epe expressway.
