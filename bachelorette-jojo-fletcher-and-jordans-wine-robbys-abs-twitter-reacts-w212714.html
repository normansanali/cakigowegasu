Bachelorette JoJo Fletcher and Jordan's Wine, Robby's Abs: Twitter Reacts

Got off on the wrong foot? The Bachelorette‘s Monday, July 11, episode included JoJo Fletcher and Jordan Rodgers drinking wine that was definitely not approved by the FDA, James throwing ab-baring Robby under the bus for his supposed roving eye, and JoJo learning that she won’t become besties with Olivia Munn anytime soon. And of course, Twitter weighed in on all of it! 

Season-long front-runner Rodgers was the center of the episode’s key scenes, as he shared a (rather gross) tipple of wine made from grapes that he and the Bachelorette smashed with their bare feet. It was enough to make anyone switch permanently to beer.

Rodgers, who has begun to lose the support of his fellow contestants, also opened up about his frosty relationship with his older brother, NFL star Aaron Rodgers, who is currently dating X-Men: Apocalypse actress Munn. 

Other key scenes included Fletcher cutting Alex after they made out atop a (possibly dead) horse, and James getting the boot after claiming that Robby checks out other women. The Bachelorette and Robby got closer than ever, despite the real estate developer grilling the former All-American swimmer on his previous long-term relationship, which had just ended mere months ago. 

Among those reacting to the episode on Twitter were season 12’s Robby, Derek and (of course) Chad Johnson, who explained what he was busy doing instead of live-tweeting every moment. Also weighing in were such Bachelor Nation faves as Sean Lowe, Ali Fedotowsky, Brooks Forester and Chris Soules.
