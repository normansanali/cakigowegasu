Rob Walton Wife: Who Is Melani Lowman-Walton?

Melani Lowman-Walton is the wife of American billionaire businessman, Rob Walton.

Melani was born in July 1974, and raised in Montana. She is Rob’s third wife whom he married in 2005. The couple live in Paradise Valley, Arizona.

Melani attended Dickinson State University, North Dakota, where she earned her first degree.

Melani Walton Career

Melani began her career in the commercial real estate sector.
She has also been a basketball coach.

Melani currently chairs the boards of the Arizona Science Center and Phoenix Children’s Hospital Foundation.

Melani Walton Philanthropy

Melani and her husband, Rob, are known for their contributions to politics. As of 2010, they had given $14,900 to Republican politicians.

Melani is deeply involved in community outreaches and and projects, as well as the Walton Family Foundation.

The foundation had donated $1 million to Dickinson State University and a portion of the school’s activities centre was named the Lowman-Walton Concourse.

The foundation, which she oversees, also makes available, grants to partners who intend to have a greater impact.
