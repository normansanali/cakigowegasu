Lindsay Greenbush Net Worth

Lindsay Greenbush was born on May 25, 1970 in Los Angeles, CA. She is an actress best known for her role as Carrie Ingalls on the television series Little House on the Prairie. She shared the role with her twin sister, Sidney Greenbush, and the two appeared on the show from 1974 to 1982.
