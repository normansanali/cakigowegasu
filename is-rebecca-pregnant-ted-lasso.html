Is Rebecca Pregnant in Ted Lasso? What Happened When Hannah Waddingham Visits Tish The Psychic?

Ted Lasso is an American sports comedy-drama television series created by Jason Sudeikis, Bill Lawrence, Brendan Hunt, and Joe Kelly. The series is based on the character Sudeikis first portrayed in a series of promotional spots for NBC Sports’ coverage of England’s Premier League.

The show follows Ted Lasso, an American college football coach who is hired to coach an English soccer team with the intention that his inexperience will lead to the team’s failure, but whose optimistic, down-to-earth leadership proves surprisingly effective.

Apple TV+ premiered the first season of ten episodes on August 14, 2020, with three episodes followed by weekly installments. A 12-episode second season premiered on July 23, 2021. The series was renewed for a third season in October 2020, which premiered on March 15, 2023.

Who Is Hannah Waddingham?

Hannah Waddingham is an English actress and singer who was born on 28 July 1974. She is best known for her role as Rebecca Welton in the comedy series Ted Lasso (2020–present), for which she was awarded the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series in 2021 and the Critics’ Choice Television Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series in 2021 and 2022.

She has also appeared in several West End productions, including Spamalot, the 2010 Regent’s Park revival of Into the Woods, and The Wizard of Oz as the Wicked Witch of the West, and has been nominated for three Olivier Awards.
