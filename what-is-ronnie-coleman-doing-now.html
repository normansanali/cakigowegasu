What Is Ronnie Coleman Doing Now?

Ronnie Coleman, born on May 13, 1964, in Bastrop, Louisiana, is a towering figure in the world of bodybuilding. Known for his eight consecutive Mr. Olympia titles from 1998 to 2005, Coleman's legacy in the sport is nothing short of iconic. But what is the legendary bodybuilder up to now, years after his retirement?

 

Ronnie Coleman's Post-Retirement

Since retiring from professional bodybuilding, Ronnie Coleman has been actively involved in various ventures that continue to showcase his passion for the sport and fitness. Despite facing numerous physical challenges due to the toll his rigorous training took on his body, Coleman has remained resilient and committed to his love for bodybuilding.

Social Media Presence
