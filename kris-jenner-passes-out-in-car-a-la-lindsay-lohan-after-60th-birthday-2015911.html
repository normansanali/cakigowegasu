Kris Jenner Passes Out in Car, a la Lindsay Lohan, After 60th Birthday

Anddd she’s down. Kris Jenner celebrated big for her 60th birthday. After being feted by her kids and friends with a massive Gatsy-themed bash at The Lot in West Hollywood on Friday, Nov. 6, the momager was photographed passed out in the back of a car on her way home — which brought to mind Lindsay Lohan’s similar pass-out from 2007.

Accompanying the sleepy mom of six was her boyfriend, Corey Gamble. 

The birthday girl’s party was an extravagant celebration. The black-tie event kicked off with a cocktail hour and also included performances by son-in-law Kanye West, as well as The Socials and The Melodies. DJ Cassidy was also on hand to spin hits. Planned by A-list party planner Mindy Weiss, the matriarch’s bash was decorated with hundreds of balloons and floral arrangements in style of the 1920s. “Ecstatic. Joyful. #blessed,” the 60-year-old captioned an Instagram of herself before her tiered cake with confetti falling from the ceiling, perfectly summing up the celebration.

Jenner stood out in a white gown with a fur shawl while her daughters Khloe, Kim, Kendall, and Kylie donned beaded designs by Yousef Al-Jasmi. Newly single Kourtney Kardashian opted for a black and white suited look.
