Biography

Jared Hasselhoff was born in Philadelphia, PA on August 5, 1971. Also known as Evil Jared, he is best known as the bassist for eclectic, alternative rock and hip hop band Bloodhound Gang. He is also a soap opera actor, known for his parts in various German soap operas. In 2009, frequent collaborator and guitarist Daniel P Carter became the lead guitar player for Bloodhound Gang after Lupus Thunder’s departure. He met Jimmy Pop at Temple University and joined him in forming a band. They released their debut album Use Your Fingers in 1995. They released One Fierce Beer Coaster in 1996, Hooray for Boobies in 2000, and Hefty Fin in 2005. Four those four albums they won an Echo award and two Comet awards.
