Trevor Booker Net Worth: How the Former NBA Player Became a Serial Entrepreneur

Trevor Booker is a former NBA player who played for five teams in eight seasons. He was known for his hustle, rebounding, and defense. He averaged 6.9 points and 5.5 rebounds per game in his career 1. But basketball is not the only thing that Booker is passionate about. He is also a serial entrepreneur who has invested in more than 12 companies and has shared interest in a half-dozen more 2. His goal is to become a billionaire through his business ventures.

Booker’s Basketball Career

Booker was born on November 25, 1987, in Newberry, South Carolina. He attended Union County High School, where he was a star basketball player. He led his team to two state championships and was named the South Carolina Player of the Year as a senior.

He then went to Clemson University, where he played for four years. He was an All-ACC first team selection as a senior and finished his college career as the second all-time leading rebounder and the sixth all-time leading scorer in Clemson history.

He was drafted by the Minnesota Timberwolves with the 23rd pick in the 2010 NBA draft, but was immediately traded to the Washington Wizards. He played for the Wizards for four seasons, then signed with the Utah Jazz in 2014. He also played for the Brooklyn Nets, the Philadelphia 76ers, and the Indiana Pacers before retiring from the NBA in 2018.

Booker’s Business Ventures

Booker has always had an interest in entrepreneurship and investing. He started his first business when he was 12 years old, selling candy at school He also learned from his father, who owned a construction company and a car wash 

He partnered with his college roommate, Jonah Baize, to form JB Fitzgerald Venture Capital, a venture capital firm that focuses on early-stage companies in various industries, such as education, technology, real estate, and sports Some of their investments include Huupe, a smart basketball hoop that allows for performance tracking and online competition; Thrive Fantasy, a daily fantasy sports platform; and The Players Trunk, an online marketplace that sells college athletes’ gear 

Booker and Baize also own accredited private high schools and basketball academies in Florida and South Carolina, where they help young athletes develop their academic and athletic skills. They also own real estate properties in several states and are involved in philanthropic initiatives, such as providing scholarships and mentoring programs for underprivileged students 

Booker has also received investments from other NBA players, such as Thaddeus Young and Spencer Dinwiddie, who share his vision of building wealth beyond basketball.

Booker’s Net Worth

According to various sources, Booker’s net worth is estimated to be between $5 million and $10 million as of 2023 . However, he has stated that his goal is to reach $1 billion through his entrepreneurial endeavors 2. He believes that he has the skills, knowledge, and network to achieve his ambition.

“I think I have what it takes,” he said. “I have a great partner who’s very smart. I have great connections. I have great mentors. I’ve learned from a lot of people. And I think I have a great eye for talent as well” .
