Career and Professional Life

Boyinaband launched his YouTube channel under the name “Boyinabandd” on November 14, 2007. His channel has altogether 311,267,801 views until now.

Most early videos on his channel were focused on his band “You and What Army” or were tutorials, educational, vlogs, reviews,  and interviews with other musicians.

Then, from 2011 onwards, parodies and skits were more prominent on the channel, for example, “How to Play Dubstep Guitar” and “Appropriate Faces for 10 Genres of Music”.

On 2 February 2015, David published the music video for his song “Don’t Stay in School”, which went viral.

In the track, he criticizes the school systems for teaching topics he deems unnecessary, instead of teaching practical life skills or topics that interest students.

Likewise, the song has been subject to controversy for seeming to encourage students to drop out of school.

However, he has stated that it is merely meant to critique the school system, and the words “Don’t Stay in School” refer to the topics discussed in the song.

Despite drawing controversy, the song has also received praise from students, teachers, and parents as well.

The video was reacted to by parents on the Fine Brothers channel, with many sympathizing with his arguments. Over time, he released a series of follow-up videos to “Don’t Stay in School”.

Likewise, his final follow-up video, “You don’t legally have to go to school”, discusses various unconventional educational options: alternative schools, charter schools, “unschooling”, and homeschooling.

Moreover, in the video, David Brown also interviews a student from one of these progressive schools.

More About Career…

David’s work spans various genres including electronic, hip-hop, and heavy metal as a producer, vocalist, rapper, songwriter, DJ, and instrumentalist.

Moreover, the band was put on hiatus in the year 2014. Other video content includes vlogs, music tutorials, education, comedy skits, raps, and challenges.

And, on August 10, 2016, he uploaded the video “I’m not Dead.”, which was a song about his problems. He then stuck to his regular upload schedule after this until October 2017.

On April 21, 2018, David Brown posted his first video in nearly six months called “My New Look.”.

This semi-update semi-comedic video revealed that he had grown his facial hair out and dyed half of the goatee red to match up with his hair to create a new look.

Likewise, on May 3rd of the same year, he shaved and revealed why he hadn’t been uploading. And, this is the first video talking about his depression since 2016. And, Brown plans on uploading normally once again.

Some of the popular videos on his YouTube channel are Don’t Stay in School, Making the Song with TheOdd1sOut (Life is Fun – BTS), Making the Song About Jaiden’s Anorexia (Empty – BTS), and The Real Reason I Have Long hair.
