Who Bianca Censori Was Before Her Marriage To Kanye West

Before her marriage to Kanye West, Bianca Censori had established herself as a talented and ambitious individual with a passion for architecture and design. Born in Melbourne, Australia, around 1995, Censori grew up in an affluent suburb known for its family-friendly atmosphere. This environment likely nurtured her appreciation for aesthetics and design from an early age​​.

  • Bianca Censori was born in Melbourne, Australia

Censori pursued her passion for architecture at the University of Melbourne, where she earned both a Bachelor of Architecture in 2017 and a Master of Architecture in 2020. Her early career included working as a student architect at DP Toscano Architects in Melbourne, gaining experience in residential architecture, including single-family homes, apartments, and commercial buildings​.

In addition to her architectural pursuits, Censori also showed an entrepreneurial spirit. She founded "Nylons Jewellery," an online venture, right after high school in 2013, showcasing her creative flair for jewelry design.

In 2020, Censori's career took a significant turn when she moved to the United States to join Yeezy, Kanye West's fashion brand. She was appointed as the Head of Architecture at Yeezy and has been credited with designing several of the brand’s retail stores, including the Yeezy Gap store in Chicago. Her work at Yeezy is characterized by clean lines, a minimalist aesthetic, and the use of innovative materials.
