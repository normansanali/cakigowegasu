Best Thing I Have Seen: WWE Fans Celebrate as Solo Sikoa Wishes His NumberOne Fan on Hi

The Bloodline is currently one of the strongest factions in the industry, and has been on a dominating streak for a long time. The faction has dominated WWE ever since their formation and has won multiple championships. Headed by The Tribal Chief, Roman Reigns, the faction consists of his cousins, Jimmy and Jey Uso, and Solo Sikoa.

Previously, the faction also consisted of Sami Zayn, however the honorary Uce was kicked out at Royal Rumble, after he turned his back on Roman Reigns. Reigns is scheduled to face Zayn next at Elimination Chamber 2023.

Solo Sikoa is the youngest member of The Bloodline, however, he has turned out to be one of the most ruthless superstars in the faction. Sikoa is known for his power and ruthlessness. Ever since his main-roster debut, the street champion has kept everyone in his path in fear.

Recently, the fearful man took to Instagram to share a loving side of his. Solo Sikoa uploaded a carousel of images and videos on Instagram, where he was seen wishing his youngest son, and his number one fan on his birthday.

The images and videos were extremely adorable, and fans did not shy away from showing their love to the set of images and videos. His son was also seen imitating his father, and fans loved that bit, and reacted to it as well.

WWE Fans react to Solo Sikoa wishing birthday to his number one fan

Although one of the most fearful men on the roster, Sikoa did not shy away from showing his love to his child. Fans were livid seeing their favorite WWE superstar showing his soft side to his child, and the fans around.
