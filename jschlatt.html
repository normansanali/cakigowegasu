Jschlatt – Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Jschlatt is a young YouTube star. He creates videos on games including Minecraft, Cities Skylines, various Wii games, and others. The young Youtube gamer is also famous for being a member of the now-defunct SMPLive. It is a stream-only Minecraft server started by CallMeCarson.

Furthermore, he created his channel on 6 July 2013. He then uploaded his first video to his channel on 6 June 2014, almost a year later after creating the channel. The first video is titled Good Boy Eats Food. This video features his pet at the time, a bearded dragon, eating food.

Moreover, he is a veteran of SMP Live. He has very iconic jokes like the infamous Schlattcoin, the “funny mic”, “what’s popping” and the “Schlatt-Walk”. He also owns 50% of the business “Schlatt & Co.” along with his most loyal business partner, ConnorEatsPants.
