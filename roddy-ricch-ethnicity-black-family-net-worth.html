Ricch has received numerous honors, including a Grammy Award after receiving nine nominations, two BET Awards, two BET Hip Hop Awards, and an American Music Award.

Rodrick began actively composing beats at the age of 16. He also served a brief time in county jail around the same time as the release of Feed the Streets II, his second commercial mixtape.
