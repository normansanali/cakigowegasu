WWE Returning to Dallas Before WM32

During tonight’s WWE Smackdown tapings in Dallas, TX, it was announced that WWE will be returning to Dallas and The American Airlines Center on October 19th for a Raw taping. This will be WWE’s final stop in Dallas before WrestleMania 32.

NXT Star & Diva Work SD! Tapings

WWE NXT stars Finn Balor and JoJo worked tonight’s Smackdown taping in Dallas. JoJo handled ring announcing duties as Eden is on her way to Glasgow for the European tour. Balor defeated Xavier Woods in a dark match tonight.
