Rollin Dank

Project Pat still releases a ton of music every year, and although he might be a little past his prime, he still reliably comes through with tracks that are undeniably great. His latest, "Rollin Dank," is one of them. It features Fetty Wap and Big Trill.

If you've ever watched an interview with Fetty, you know that his speaking voice is much different (and lower) than the high-pitched squawk he's become known for, and on "Rollin Dank," he lets his vocals slip down much closer to their usual pitch. He mumbles his way through a hook that perfectly reflects the song's blunted atmosphere, and Patta and Trill's verses do the heavy lifting elsewhere.
