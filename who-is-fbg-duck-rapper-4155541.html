Who Is FBG Duck? Five Things On Rapper Hollywood Life

UPDATE (8/5/2020, 6:35 PM EST): The Cook County Medical Examiner’s Office has determined that FBG Duck, whose real name is Carlton Weekly, died “as a result of multiple gunshot wounds.” The manner of the 26-year-old rapper’s death was deemed a homicide, and he was pronounced dead at the Northwestern Memorial Hospital in Chicago on Aug. 4, according to a statement provided to HollywoodLife.

UPDATE (8/5/2020, 1:23 AM EST): The Cook County Medical Examiner’s Office confirmed the death of 26-year-old rapper FBG Duck with HollywoodLife. “The Cook County Medical Examiner’s Office has been notified of the death of 26-year-old Carlton Weekly [FBG Duck’s real name] of the 16100 block of South Justine Street in Markham, Ill. The incident occurred in the 0 – 100 block of East Oak Street in Chicago. Weekly was pronounced dead today at 5:06 p.m. at Northwestern Memorial Hospital in Chicago. An update will be provided once an autopsy has been scheduled,” read a statement provided by Natalia Derevyanny, the director of communications at the Cook County Bureau of Administration. However, the police have yet to identify the victims from the reported drive-by shooting that allegedly took FBG Duck’s life (more on that below).

ORIGINAL: FBG Duck, 26, whose real name is Carlton D. Weekly, was reportedly one of three people who were shot in a drive-by shooting in Chicago’s Gold Coast neighborhood on the afternoon of Aug. 4, according to ABC7 Chicago. Friends and people who knew the rapper also claimed he died in the shooting after being brought to the city’s Northwestern Hospital, the outlet reported. Although the Chicago Police Department has yet to identify the victims of the shooting, Daniel O’Shea, the Deputy Chief for the department, confirmed during a press conference on Tuesday evening that a shooting did happen.

“At approximately 16:37 hours today, right here in the 100 block of East Oak Street, we had three individuals shopping,” O’Shea explained in the conference. “Two vehicles pulled up. Four individuals exited those vehicles, two from each vehicle, and began firing at the individuals that were on the sidewalk outside of one of the stores here. They also struck a female that was in one of the vehicles, which was also parked outside one of the stores here.”

“Right now we have one male, Black, 26 years old, transported to Northwestern Hospital, where he was pronounced deceased,” he continued. “We have one female, Black, 26 years old, who was also transported to a hospital. She’s currently in serious condition. And we have an additional male, Black, 36 years old, also in serious condition at Northwestern Hospital.”

HollywoodLife has reached out to FBG Duck’s rep to confirm he was one of the victims.

Here are five things about FBG Duck and the shocking situation that you should know.

1. He’s from Chicago. The talented artist gained recognition for being a member of the rap group Fly Boy Gang, which is where the abbreviation “FBG” in his name came from, aka Clout Boyz. All the members in the group are from Chicago and mostly from the Gangster Disciples, a gang from the South Side of Chicago that was started in the 1960s. They include FBG Duck, Lil Jay, Cashout, Billionaire Black, Young Mello, and Dutchie.
