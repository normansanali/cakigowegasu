Who is Tom Selleck?

He was raised in Detroit, Michigan, and joined the California Army National Guard in 1967. He was born on January 29, 1945. He has two kids and is wed to Jillie Mack. He is also a spokesman for the National Rifle Association and a co-founder of the Character Counts! group.
