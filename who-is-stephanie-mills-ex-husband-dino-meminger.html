Who Is Stephanie Mills Ex-Husband Dino Meminger?

Stephanie Dorthea Mills was born on March 22, 1957, in Bedford–Stuyvesant, Brooklyn, New York, the U.S. Stephanie Mills is a Grammy award-winning singer, songwriter, actress, and Broadway star who started singing at the age of three. Stephanie Mills became well known as “Dorothy” in the original 7 Time Tony Award Winning Broadway run of the musical The Wiz from 1974 to 1979.

Mills is on record to have married three times

    • Jeffrey Daniel married in 1980 and divorced in 1983.
  • Dino Meminger, married in 1989 and divorced in 1991.
  • Michael Saunders, married in 1993 and divorced in 2001.

Mills has a son named Farad Mills. Farad was born with Down Syndrome.

Steph also confirmed she dated the late Michael Jackson for a year and a half.

Who Is Stephanie Mills’ Ex-Husband Dino Meminger?

Dino Meminger was born on February 16, 1956. Dino is best known for his work on The Tomorrow Man (1996), Profiler (1996), and Brimstone (1998). Unfortunately, Dino died on January 17, 2004.
