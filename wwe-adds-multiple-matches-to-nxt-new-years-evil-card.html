WWE Adds Multiple Matches To NXT New Years Evil Card

WWE NXT’s next special event, New Year’s Evil, will take place in the Performance Center in Orlando on January 2nd. That being said, it is still set to be a big card, and now we have more matches for the show.

Dragon Lee, Joaquin Wilde, Cruz Del Toro vs No Quarter Catch Club was added to New Year’s Evil. The No Quarter Catch Club cut an intense backstage promo to hype the match.

Arianna Grace had a backstage segment later on with Ava, who is apparently now an authority figure on NXT. The Rock’s daughter listened to Arianna Grace’s complaint about Roxanne Perez, and then she revealed that they will have a match at New Year’s Evil.

This week on WWE NXT, the official confirmation came in that Ilja Dragunov is set to defend the NXT Title against Trick Williams. Furthermore, there’s a championship clash on the horizon as Lyra Valkyria is gearing up to defend her title against Blair Davenport at New Year’s Evil.

Tiffany Stratton and Fallon Henley are showing no signs of settling their differences. In the latest NXT episode, the tension between them escalated, leading to Stratton cutting a backstage promo on Henley. In the aftermath, she went on to book a singles match between them at New Year’s Evil.

Riley Osborne vs. Oba Femi will also go down at NXT New Year’s Evil in the Men’s Breakout Tournament Final.

We will have full coverage of New Year’s Evil, and everything leading toward the event, right here at Ringside News. Keep checking back for more updates!

WWE NXT New Year’s Evil Card:

Ilja Dragunov (c) vs. Trick Williams – WWE NXT Title

Lyra Valkyria (c) vs. Blair Davenport – WWE NXT Women’s Title

Tiffany Stratton vs Fallon Henley

Riley Osborne vs. Oba Femi – Men’s Breakout Tournament Final

Dragon Lee, Joaquin Wilde, Cruz Del Toro vs No Quarter Catch Club

Arianna Grace vs Roxanne Perez

Ridge Holland will speak on hurting Ilja Dragunov
