Stanley Jordan is a jazz fusion guitarist and pianist born on July 31, 1959 in Chicago, IL. He is best known for his unique two-handed tapping technique, which allows him to play both guitar and piano simultaneously. His style has been influential in the jazz fusion genre, and he has released several albums throughout his career.
