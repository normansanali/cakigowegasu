Lindsay Lohan Is Already Showing and So Happy amid Pregnancy News, Her Mom Says

Lindsay Lohan's mother could not be happier for the actress amid her journey to motherhood.

In a new interview with People, Dina Lohan voiced her excitement over the Freaky Friday star's pregnancy news and revealed Lindsay has always wanted to be a mother.

"I'm literally over the moon. I'm so happy, I can't stop smiling," Dina told the magazine. "It's incredible. We're just so excited. It's just such a beautiful thing for my child! And I love babies. Who doesn't?"
