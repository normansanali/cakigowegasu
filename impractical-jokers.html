'Impractical Jokers' Latest News and Updates

The hidden camera reality show Impractical Jokers follows a group of childhood friends as they compete in a series of dares and pranks often involving members of the public. The "loser" of each episode is then subjected to a "punishment," resulting in peak embarrassment. 

The Jokers — Q, Murr, Joe, and Sal — are also members of the sketch comedy troupe The Tenderloins, and often tour across the country to perform for fans live. The show also got the Jokers their own game show called Misery Index.
