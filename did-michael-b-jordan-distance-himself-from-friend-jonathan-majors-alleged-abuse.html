Did Michael B. Jordan Distance Himself From Friend Jonathan Majors Since His Scandals Came To Light?

Earlier this year, Hollywood rising star Jonathan Majors was arrested and accused of severe allegations by several women. This news sent shockwaves through the industry and left many wondering about the future of his career. Among those affected was Michael B. Jordan, who had been friends with Majors for years.

While neither party has confirmed or denied these rumors, it's clear that their relationship has been strained in recent months. Here's everything to know about the ongoing situation thus far.

Everything To Know About Michael B. Jordan And Jonathan Majors Friendship

Jordan and Majors are two of the most talented actors in Hollywood today. Both have made a name for themselves in recent years, with Jordan starring in blockbuster hits like Creed and Black Panther.

Majors has wowed audiences with his performances in The Last Black Man in San Francisco and Lovecraft Country. However, what many people may not know is that these two actors are actually best friends.

Both had grown up in modest circumstances – Jordan in Newark, New Jersey, and Majors in Dallas, Texas – and both were determined to make it big in Hollywood despite facing numerous obstacles along the way. When they finally got to know each other while filming for Creed 3, they bonded over their shared struggles as well as their passion for acting.

These two actors starred together in the movie as Adonis Creed and Kenny Porter, respectively. According to The New York Times, their friendship began when they first met during rehearsals for Creed 3. They quickly hit it off and spent time together both on and off-set. The chemistry between them was palpable on screen as they played their roles with ease.

What Was Jonathan Majors Accused Of In 2023?

The entertainment industry has been rocked by the allegations against Majors. These allegations came to light in 2023, causing a massive uproar among fans and fellow actors alike. According to reports, several women have accused Majors of personal harassment and inappropriate behavior on set. While the exact details of these allegations are not yet known, they have already caused significant damage to Majors' reputation and career.

On March 25th, 2023, Majors was arrested in New York after his girlfriend called the police on him. According to Vox, his unnamed lover was bruised and has since filed a restraining order on Majors.

Further accusations against Majors have been made that are serious and alarming.

According to Variety, several women have come forward with stories of how they experienced inappropriate behavior from the actor during auditions or on set. Some alleged that he physically hurt them while others accused him of making unwanted romantic advances towards them. These allegations bring into focus the issue of accountability for questionable behavior in the entertainment industry.

It really is mind-blowing how Majors went from being so beloved for his incredible acting and dedication to health and fitness, all the way to being someone so problematic and disliked. How has Jordan reacted to this swing in perception?

What Does Michael B. Jordan Think About Jonathan Major's Accusations?
