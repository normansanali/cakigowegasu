Elise Ecklund - Bio, Career, Single, Age, Net Worth, Height, Facts

Table of Biography

Elise Ecklund is an American guitar and ukulele player. Elise Ecklund is famous for her self-titled YouTube channel where she posts covers and music videos. On her YouTube channel, she also posts content related to beauty, lifestyle, and comedy.

Early Life and Childhood

American guitarist Elise Ecklund was born on February 18, 1997, in Wichita, Kansas. She is 28 years old as of 2024 and her zodiac sign is Aquarius. Likewise, she spent her childhood in the city of Bartlesville. Ecklund later moved to Oklahoma with her family. She belongs to the American nationality and her ethnicity is Caucasian.

In her videos, she has featured her mother and father but she has not revealed their names yet. Regarding her siblings, she has altogether five of them; two sisters, and three brothers. And she frequently collabs with her sister Eden. She has collaborated on a channel with Eden called “Eden and Elise”.

Her sister Eden is also a YouTube star and has a YouTube channel “Eden Ecklund”. She has even taught her sister Eden and her mother to play the ukulele in the past. Talking about her education she graduated from John Brown University and Southern New Hampshire University with a degree in Marketing.

She and her sister Eden moved out of her parent’s house in Bartlesville, Oklahoma in 2019. The sister moved into an apartment in Tulsa, Oklahoma, along with their dog, Sunshine. In 2020, both Ecklund sisters moved into a new house with Sunshine after a carbon monoxide incident in the apartment building.

Elise Ecklund – Professional Life

Elise Ecklund gave a start to her career as a YouTuber. She opened her self-titled YouTube channel ‘Elise Ecklund‘ on February 12, 2015. She posted her video ‘Youth – Troye Sivan – Easy Guitar Tutorial,’ where she taught her audience the basic steps for learning guitar for beginners. Later she posted some acoustic covers of House of Memories – Panic- At The Disco and also ukulele covers by which she came to the limelight.

Likewise, she posted a challenge-based video on July 19, 2016, which was titled ‘Yoga Challenge w/ Anna Gibson’, with her friend Anna Gibson. In the year 2017, she posted a ‘4 Basic Chords, 40 Songs on Ukulele’ video which was a great hit and the video became a hit within a week with more than a million views.

Caption: Elise Ecklund on her YouTube video (Source: YouTube)

Through the video ‘4 basic chords, 40 songs on ukulele’ her Youtube channel was boosted and she achieved a huge fan following. Some of her most popular videos are ‘I Wrote a Song Using Only Your Instagram Comments‘ and ‘I Wrote a Song Using Only Hate Comments,’ which are worth watching!

As of now, her YouTube channel has 1.65M subscribers with 474 video uploads. Not only Youtube, but she is also currently serving as a marketing official at Amazon in addition to managing her YouTube career. She holds the world record for writing the shortest song to ever be officially released; ‘I Suffer More,’ released on May 31st, 2019.

Music

Her channel started with ukelele and guitar tutorials of songs by famous music artists such as Twenty-One Pilots, Melanie Martinez, and Halsey. Later she expanded her range to artists like Billie Eilish, Taylor Swift, Ed Sheeran, and Panic at the Disco.

Her YouTube channel started to grow, and Elise started writing songs using comments from her Twitter account, YouTube comments (both positive and negative) Instagram comments, and related concepts. She has even used an online lyric generator to compose joke songs for her to sing, sniffed helium to make her voice higher as another joke, and has done a couple of speed-songwriting videos. She follows Kidz Bop and there are multiple videos on her YouTube regarding it.

Likewise, she has written many officially released songs, including I Hope My Ex Is Happy—an emo song about break ups and romance, I Suffer More—the world’s shortest song,  Oof—a song written over the ‘of’ Sound Effects from Roblox, which is a recurring joke on her channel, and many more. She also shares her official songs on iTunes and Spotify.
