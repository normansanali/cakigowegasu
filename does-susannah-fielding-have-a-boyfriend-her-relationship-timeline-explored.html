Does Susannah Fielding Have A Boyfriend?

From 2008 until 2011, Fielding dated English actor Tom Hiddleston. They met on the set of the TV show Wallander.

Fielding was born in Poole, England, and grew up in Havant, near Portsmouth, before attending Christ’s Hospital School in Sussex for two years, when she discovered her passion for acting.

She also went to Dynamo Youth Theatre in Havant, which is a youth theatre for kids aged 11 to 18.

Susannah studied at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama, where she played Trinculo in The Tempest and Myrrah in Tales of Ovid, among other roles. In 2006, she received her Guildhall diploma.
