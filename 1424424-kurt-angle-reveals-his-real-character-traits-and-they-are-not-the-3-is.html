Kurt Angle Reveals His Real Character Traits And They Are Not The 3 Is

Former WWE World Heavyweight Champion is one of the most popular wrestlers of all time. He had been one of the top stars in the Stamford-based promotion during the Attitude Era and the Ruthless Aggression Era. The WWE Hall of Famer shared what his real traits are and those are not what his professional wrestling character was known for.

Although fans chant ‘You Suck’ whenever Kurt Angle makes his entrance, The Olympic Gold Medalist was one of the stars that the audience absolutely loved and adored. As part of the character that he played in WWE, he had a few traits that defined who he was. Those were Intensity, Integrity, and Intelligence. In short, they were called the 3I’s.

Recently, the former WWE World Heavyweight Champion shared what his real-life character traits were. They were hope, faith, and love. Angle mentioned that he was a firm believer in the Almighty Jesus Christ.

“Most people know me by my 3 character traits… the 3 Is’…intensity, integrity and intelligence. But my real traits are Hope, Faith and Love. I’m a believer that JESUS died so that we could live. And I live for HIM. And that is true, it’s “darn” true -Kurt Angle,” The Olympic Gold Medalist said.

Apart from Kurt Angle being one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time, the WWE Hall of Famer is also popularly known for winning the Gold Medal in Freestyle Wrestling at the 1996 Summer Olympics. The five-time WWE World Heavyweight Champion did so with a broken neck.

Angle has retired from professional wrestling. His last match was against Baron Corbin at WrestleMania 35.
