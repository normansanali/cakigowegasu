The Meaning Behind The Song: One Woman Man (Remix) by Dave Hollister

The song “One Woman Man (Remix)” by Dave Hollister is a heartfelt ode to monogamy and commitment in relationships. Released as part of his album “Chicago ’85… The Movie” in 2000, it explores the growth and maturity within a person who transitions from a player to a faithful partner.

Listening to the lyrics, we are instantly transported back to a time of wild and carefree experiences. Dave Hollister describes running into an old flame after three years, triggering memories of their passionate and spontaneous encounters. He reminisces about a time when he didn’t care about the consequences of his actions and was more focused on his own pleasure.

However, as the song progresses, we see a change in perspective. Hollister acknowledges that everything is different now. He highlights how he has settled down and become a “one woman man.” He reflects on the many encounters he had after the relationship with his former flame ended, but realizes that finding the right person who makes him feel like no other is a blessing.

The lyrics convey a sense of gratitude and contentment for the commitment he has made. He praises the person he is now dedicated to but acknowledges the attraction towards his old flame, stating that she looks good as hell. However, his loyalty lies with his current partner, and he cannot go home with his former flame because he is committed to going home to the person he loves.

The remix also features a rap verse from Redman, adding an extra layer to the song. Redman playfully discusses his own experience with commitment and the transformative power of love. He emphasizes how his perspective has changed since being with his partner. Redman’s verse further underscores the theme of growth and transformation in relationships.

Personally, this song holds a special place in my heart. It serves as a reminder of the evolution we go through in relationships and the choices we make to prioritize love and commitment. It resonates with my own journey of transitioning from a carefree and wild time to valuing stability and faithfulness.

“One Woman Man (Remix)” showcases the power of love to transform us into better versions of ourselves. It emphasizes the importance of loyalty, commitment, and the ability to appreciate what we have rather than constantly seeking new experiences. Dave Hollister’s soulful vocals and heartfelt lyrics make this song a timeless treasure that continues to resonate with listeners.

In conclusion, “One Woman Man (Remix)” by Dave Hollister is a song that reflects personal growth, maturity, and the decision to commit to one person. It highlights the experiences that shaped the artist’s outlook on love and relationships while reminding us of the beauty and strength that comes from being faithful to someone we love.
